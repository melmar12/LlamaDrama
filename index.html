Why Llamas?

Guardian

Protect your herd! Llamas are excellent guard animals. You can always rely on the llama's instinctual ability to detect potential danger! Llama owners report great reductions in predator loss.

Warmth

Llama fur is like no other. Their fleece sheds moisture, stays warm when wet, is wind-resistant and dries quickly. It is naturally breathable, durable, flame resistant and self-extinguishing. Great for fleece garments!

Companion

Llamas are actually just dogs in llama bodies. They are very friendly, intelligent and are safe around children. They are also know for their cleanliness and are low maintenance. They make great pets!

Cargo

Going on a golfing trip? Bring your llama! They can easily carry about 25% of their body weight. Careful not to overload your llama though, llamas are sassy and may refuse to move or even spit at you!

Intelligence

Llamas are smart dude. This guy taught his to surf. Other llamas have learned to jump through hoops, give kisses, and roll over. Who knows what else they can do, maybe you and your llama can be internet famous someday.

Because Why Not?
